Håkan Hardenberger - Malmö Live
Håkan Hardenberger is "the best trumpet player in the world" -The Times

The first person to make me aware of Håkan Hardenberger was my brother. This must have been around 1985 since he was making military service that year as a musician. Håkan was also among the first in the 80’s to do military service as a wind band soldier. I remember being very admiring about hearing him play. It was fantastic.

Not long after that I heard Håkans first recording in his own name, ”The Virtuoso trumpet”. Especially the playing in Jean Françaix Sonatina and the Variations on a Theme from Norma had big impact on me. Such smooth, precise and musically playing. I was astounded and very happy to hear such fine trumpet playing.

I have followed Håkan since and visited a couple of concerts. Always happy to hear this wonderful music flowing out from him and his trumpet. Very inspirational for me
